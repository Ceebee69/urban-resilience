Our Mission

Built in Chicago.
Built for everyone.

PrepWise started from a simple observation: most emergency kits are either cheap, incomplete, or so tactical they feel irrelevant to everyday life in a city or suburb.

We set out to fix that — building FEMA-aligned kits that work whether you're in a high-rise in Chicago or a ranch home in the suburbs. Every kit is hand-packed, every item chosen for real-world utility, and every price point designed to make preparedness accessible.

We're not selling fear. We're selling readiness — the quiet confidence that comes from knowing that whatever happens in the next 6 hours, 72 hours, or 30 days, your household is covered.
